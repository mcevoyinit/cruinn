The process

From recording to certified transcript

Every transcript follows the same controlled path. Machine speed where it helps, trained human judgement where it matters, and a cryptographic seal at the end so the result can be relied upon.

  1. 1

    Recording received

    The interview recording is handed over through a secure channel with a logged, auditable point of receipt.

  2. 2

    AI transcription, tuned for real accents

    A private speech model produces a first pass transcript, tuned to handle Irish and regional accents and difficult room audio.

  3. 3

    Human certifies every line

    A trained, vetted reviewer checks and corrects the full record, then certifies its accuracy against the audio.

  4. 4

    Cryptographically sealed

    The certified transcript is sealed so that any later change, however small, is mathematically detectable.

  5. 5

    Certified transcript delivered

    You receive a clean, certified document with a verifiable seal and a complete chain of custody behind it.

Why it can be trusted

Records you can stand over

A transcript used as a formal record has to be defensible. Cruinn is built so that integrity, provenance, and confidentiality are properties of the system, not promises on a page.

Tamper evident seal

Each certified transcript carries a cryptographic seal. If a single character is altered, the seal no longer verifies, so any change is detectable by anyone who checks it.

Full chain of custody

Every step, from receipt of the recording to delivery of the transcript, is logged. The provenance of the document can be demonstrated end to end.

Data stays in Ireland

Recordings and transcripts are processed and stored within Ireland. Sensitive material stays under Irish jurisdiction throughout.

Private local models

Transcription runs on private models hosted on our own infrastructure. No interview audio is sent to a third party cloud service.

Vetted reviewers

Reviewers are trained and security vetted for work on sensitive and confidential material, and operate under strict confidentiality.

Data protection by design

Designed to align with GDPR and applicable data protection law, with data minimisation and access controls built in.
